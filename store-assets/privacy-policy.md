# Privacy Policy — DRACO NATION

**Last updated:** May 2026  
**Developer:** Andrew Ayuba  
**Contact:** AndrewAyuba25@gmail.com

## 1. Data Collection
DRACO NATION does **not** collect, transmit, or store any personal data on
external servers. All information — including generated numbers, verification
codes, and message logs — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage
and never leaves your device.

## 2. Local Storage
We use device local storage to save your virtual numbers and activity history
between sessions. You can clear this at any time through your browser or device
settings, or using the in-app "Clear All" buttons.

## 3. Third-Party Services
Optional donation links open PayPal's website in a new tab. PayPal's own
privacy policy governs any data collected during that transaction. DRACO NATION
does not receive or process any payment information.

## 4. No Tracking
DRACO NATION contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, cookies, tracking pixels,
or crash reporters of any kind. Your usage is entirely private.

## 5. Virtual Numbers
Numbers generated by this app are virtual and for verification simulation
purposes only. They are not real telephone numbers and cannot send or receive
actual SMS messages from external networks.

## 6. Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 7. Changes
Any updates to this policy will be reflected in the app and on this page with
a new "Last updated" date.
