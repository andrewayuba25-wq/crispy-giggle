How to Use

  1. Generate a Number — Go to Virtual Numbers, pick a country & city, click Generate.
  2. Save the Number — Click Save Number to keep it for reuse.
  3. Verify an App — Go to SMS Verify or Social Media, select your number, click Request Code.
  4. Use the Code — Copy the 6-digit code, paste it into the app/website, click Confirm.
  5. Check Messages — All SMS activity is logged in the Messages tab.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data Collection

DRACO NATION does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All information — including generated numbers, verification codes, and message logs — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device.

2. Local Storage

We use localStorage to save your virtual numbers and activity history so that data persists between sessions. You can clear this data at any time by using your browser's built-in storage clearing tools, or by using the "Clear All" buttons within the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The optional donation links open PayPal's website in a new tab. PayPal's own privacy policy governs any data collected during that transaction. DRACO NATION does not receive or process any payment data.

4. No Tracking

DRACO NATION contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, cookies, or tracking pixels of any kind. Your usage is entirely private.

5. Virtual Numbers

The phone numbers generated by this app are virtual and for verification simulation purposes only. They are not real telephone numbers and cannot send or receive actual SMS messages.
